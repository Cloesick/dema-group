### Month 6-8: Assessment & Requirements

| Week | Action | Owner | Deliverable |

**Key Questions to Answer:**
- What ERP systems are currently in use?
- What are the critical integrations needed?

### Month 9-10: Selection & Planning

| Week | Action | Owner | Deliverable |

**ERP Options to Evaluate:**

| System | Pros | Cons | Est. Cost |
|--------|------|------|-----------|
| **Odoo** | Open source, flexible, good for SME | Less robust for complex manufacturing | €30-50K |
| **SAP Business One** | Industry standard, robust | Higher cost, complexity | €80-120K |
| **Microsoft Dynamics 365** | Good integration with Office | Learning curve | €60-100K |
| **Exact Online** | Popular in Benelux, cloud-native | Limited customization | €40-60K |

### Consolidation Options

| Option | Description | Savings | Investment |
|--------|-------------|---------|------------|
| **A: Full consolidation** | Single warehouse | €150K/year | €200K |
| **B: Hub & spoke** | Central + satellites | €80K/year | €100K |
| **C: Optimize existing** | Keep separate, improve | €40K/year | €30K |

**Recommendation:** Option B (Hub & Spoke) - Balance of savings and risk

## Workstream 5: Customer Migration

### Migration Strategy

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                 CUSTOMER MIGRATION WAVES                     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                              │
│  Wave 1 (Month 12-14): Key Accounts                         │
│  ├── Top 50 customers by revenue                            │
│  ├── Personal onboarding                                    │
│  └── Target: 20% of customers, 50% of revenue              │
│                                                              │
│  Wave 2 (Month 14-16): Medium Accounts                      │
│  ├── Next 200 customers                                     │
│  ├── Group onboarding sessions                              │
│  └── Target: 40% of customers, 35% of revenue              │
│                                                              │
│  Wave 3 (Month 16-18): All Remaining                        │
│  ├── Self-service migration                                 │
│  ├── Email campaign + support                               │
│  └── Target: 40% of customers, 15% of revenue              │
│                                                              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Migration Checklist (Per Customer)

- [ ] Account created on new platform
- [ ] Historical orders imported
- [ ] Pricing tier assigned
- [ ] Credit terms configured
- [ ] Primary contact trained
- [ ] First order placed on new platform
- [ ] Old account deactivated

### Customer Communication Plan

| Timing | Communication | Channel |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| -4 weeks | Announcement of new platform | Email + Letter |
| -2 weeks | Migration date notification | Email |
| -1 week | Login credentials | Email |
| Migration day | Welcome + tutorial | Email + Call |
| +1 week | Check-in | Phone |
| +2 weeks | Feedback request | Email |
