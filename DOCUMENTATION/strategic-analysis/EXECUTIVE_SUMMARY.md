# DEMA Group Digital Transformation - Executive Summary

## Current State
DEMA Group consists of five specialized companies:
- **DEMA**: Industrial pumps and equipment
- **Fluxer**: Process flow and valve technology
- **Beltz247**: Conveyor systems and maintenance
- **De Visschere**: Irrigation systems
- **Accu**: Precision components

The ongoing digital transformation project aims to unify these companies under a single digital platform while maintaining their individual strengths and market positions.
