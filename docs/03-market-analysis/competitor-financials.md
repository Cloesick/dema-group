## Strategic Implications

### Opportunities

1. **TVH Partnership**
   - TVH lacks pump/irrigation expertise
   - Potential supplier or referral relationship
   - 15 km proximity enables collaboration

2. **Kramp Dealer Status**
   - Kramp needs local dealers
   - DEMA has agricultural customer base
   - Complementary product ranges

3. **Niche Focus**
   - Avoid direct competition with giants
   - Focus on pumps, irrigation, agricultural
   - Build expertise competitors lack

4. **Digital Differentiation**
   - Most competitors have basic e-commerce
   - AI-powered product assistant is differentiator
   - Modern platform can attract customers

### Threats

1. **TVH Expansion**
   - If TVH enters pumps seriously, major threat
   - Their scale and logistics are unmatched

2. **Rubix Pricing**
   - Can undercut on price due to scale
   - Focus on value-add services to compete

3. **Online Pure-Players**
   - Amazon Business, Manutan
   - Compete on expertise and service

## Data Limitations

1. **Paid Data Required**
   - Full jaarrekeningen require paid access (Trendstop, CompanyWeb)
   - NBB Balanscentrale has free basic data

2. **Holding Structures**
   - TVH has complex structure (Parts, Holding, Global)
   - Revenue may be consolidated differently

3. **Private Companies**
   - Limited disclosure requirements for smaller companies
   - Some figures are estimates

4. **Currency/Timing**
   - All figures in EUR
   - Most recent available data (2023-2024)
