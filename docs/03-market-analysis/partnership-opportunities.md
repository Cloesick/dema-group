# Partnership & Collaboration Opportunities

This document identifies strategic partnership opportunities for DEMA Group within the 100km radius of West Flanders and the French border region.

## Partnership Categories

### 1. Distribution Partnerships

#### TVH Group (Waregem) - High Priority ⭐

**Profile:**
- €1.5B+ revenue, 5,000+ employees
- 15 km from DEMA
- Focus: Material handling, industrial, construction, agriculture
- Gap: Limited pump/irrigation expertise

**Partnership Model Options:**

| Model | Description | Benefit to DEMA | Benefit to TVH |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Supplier** | DEMA supplies pumps to TVH | Access to TVH's 180-country network | Fill product gap |
| **White-label** | DEMA products under TVH brand | Volume, no marketing cost | Expand offering |
| **Referral** | TVH refers pump inquiries | Qualified leads | Customer satisfaction |
| **Co-location** | DEMA counter at TVH | Foot traffic | Complete offering |

**Action Items:**
1. Identify TVH business development contact
2. Prepare partnership proposal
3. Schedule meeting Q1 2025

## Partnership Development Process

### Stage 1: Identification (Week 1-2)
- Research potential partners
- Identify key contacts
- Assess strategic fit

### Stage 2: Initial Contact (Week 3-4)
- Reach out to contacts
- Schedule introductory meetings
- Share company overview

### Stage 3: Exploration (Week 5-8)
- Detailed discussions
- Site visits
- Identify synergies

### Stage 4: Proposal (Week 9-10)
- Draft partnership proposal
- Define terms and conditions
- Internal approval

### Stage 5: Negotiation (Week 11-14)
- Negotiate terms
- Legal review
- Finalize agreement

### Stage 6: Implementation (Week 15+)
- Sign agreement
- Announce partnership
- Begin operations

## Risk Mitigation

### Partnership Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Partner becomes competitor** | Non-compete clauses |
| **Quality issues** | Quality standards in agreement |
| **Dependency** | Diversify partnerships |
| **Cultural mismatch** | Due diligence, trial period |
| **Financial instability** | Credit checks, guarantees |

### Exit Strategies

| Scenario | Exit Strategy |
|----------|---------------|
| **Underperformance** | 90-day notice clause |
| **Strategic change** | Buyout option |
| **Dispute** | Mediation clause |
