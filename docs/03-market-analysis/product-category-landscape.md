## Recommendations

### Product Portfolio Actions

1. **Expand:**
   - Dosing pumps (ProMinent, Grundfos)
   - Stainless steel fittings
   - Water treatment products
   - Booster systems

2. **Strengthen:**
   - Irrigation systems (complete solutions)
   - Agricultural hydraulics
   - Compressed air accessories

3. **Consider:**
   - Industrial valves (partnership with Sodeco?)
   - Pneumatic components
   - Safety equipment (bundle)

4. **Avoid:**
   - Heavy industrial hydraulics (HYDAC territory)
   - Premium tool brands (DeWalt, Milwaukee)
   - Large industrial compressors
