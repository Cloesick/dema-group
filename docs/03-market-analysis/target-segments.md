## Detailed Segment Profiles

### 1. Agriculture & Horticulture ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

#### Segment Characteristics
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Customer Types** | Farmers, greenhouse growers, agricultural contractors |
| **Geography** | West Flanders, East Flanders, Limburg (intensive farming) |
| **Buying Behavior** | Seasonal peaks, price-sensitive, relationship-driven |
| **Decision Makers** | Farm owners, technical managers |
| **Purchase Cycle** | Annual planning + emergency purchases |

#### Needs & Pain Points
| Need | Pain Point | Our Solution |
|------|------------|--------------|
| Irrigation systems | Complex to design, multiple suppliers | DVT design + DEMA products |
| Pump reliability | Downtime = crop loss | 24/7 service, quality brands |
| Spare parts availability | Long lead times | Local stock, fast delivery |
| Technical advice | Lack of expertise | Trained sales team |
| Price competitiveness | Kramp price pressure | Volume discounts, bundles |

#### Product Fit by Company
| Company | Products | Relevance |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| DEMA | Pumps, pipes, irrigation, hoses | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| De Visschere | Irrigation design & install | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Fluxer | Control valves, automation | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Accu | Fasteners for equipment | ⭐⭐ |

#### Go-to-Market Strategy
1. **Channel:** Direct sales + dealer network
2. **Marketing:** Trade shows (Agribex), farm magazines, field days
3. **Pricing:** Competitive with Kramp, bundle discounts
4. **Service:** Seasonal service contracts, emergency response
