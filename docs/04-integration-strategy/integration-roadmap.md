## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 0-6)

### Objectives
1. Establish governance structure
2. Unify product data
3. Launch unified e-commerce platform
4. Define brand strategy

### Workstreams

#### 1.1 Governance & Organization
| Task | Owner | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Form Integration Steering Committee | CEO Group | Week 1-2 | Charter document |
| Appoint Integration Manager | Steering Committee | Week 2 | Job description, hire |
| Define decision rights | Integration Manager | Week 3-4 | RACI matrix |
| Establish communication plan | Integration Manager | Week 4 | Communication calendar |
| Legal structure review | External counsel | Week 4-8 | Recommendations |

#### 1.2 Product Data Unification
| Task | Owner | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Inventory all product catalogs | Each company | Week 1-4 | Product count per company |
| Define unified data schema | Tech lead | Week 2-4 | Data dictionary |
| Map products to categories | Product team | Week 4-8 | Category hierarchy |
| Clean and normalize data | Product team | Week 6-12 | Unified product database |
| Import to platform | Tech lead | Week 10-16 | Live catalog |

**Target:** 50,000+ SKUs in unified catalog

#### 1.3 E-Commerce Platform
| Task | Owner | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Extend DEMA webshop codebase | Dev team | Week 1-4 | Technical assessment |
| Add multi-company support | Dev team | Week 4-12 | Platform update |
| Implement unified search | Dev team | Week 8-16 | AI-powered search |
| Customer account system | Dev team | Week 12-20 | B2B portal |
| Payment/invoicing integration | Dev team | Week 16-24 | Checkout flow |

**Leverage:** Existing DEMA webshop with AI chatbot

#### 1.4 Brand Strategy
| Task | Owner | Timeline | Deliverable |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Brand audit | Marketing | Week 1-4 | Current state analysis |
| Customer research | Marketing | Week 4-8 | Survey results |
| Brand architecture decision | Steering Committee | Week 8-10 | Decision document |
| Visual identity development | Agency | Week 10-20 | Brand guidelines |
| Launch plan | Marketing | Week 20-24 | Go-to-market plan |

**Options:**
- A) Unified new brand (e.g., "IndustriaGroup")
- B) Endorsed brands (e.g., "DEMA, a [Group] company")
- C) House of brands (keep all, unified platform)

### Phase 1 Milestones

| Milestone | Target Date | Success Criteria |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| M1: Governance established | Month 1 | Steering committee active |
| M2: Data schema defined | Month 2 | All companies aligned |
| M3: Platform MVP | Month 4 | Internal testing |
| M4: Brand decision | Month 3 | Approved by all owners |
| M5: Platform launch | Month 6 | Public beta |

## Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Customer churn during transition | Medium | High | Phased migration, dedicated support |
| Technical integration failures | Medium | High | Proven platform (DEMA), experienced team |
| Cultural resistance | High | Medium | Change management, communication |
| Key employee departure | Medium | High | Retention packages, knowledge transfer |
| Budget overrun | Medium | Medium | Contingency reserves, phased approach |
| Competitor response | Low | Medium | Speed of execution |
