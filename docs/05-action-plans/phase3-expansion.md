## Workstream 1: Geographic Expansion

### Market Analysis (Month 18-22)

| Market | Attractiveness | Entry Difficulty | Priority |
|--------|---------------|------------------|----------|
| **Netherlands** | High | Medium | 1st |
| **Luxembourg** | Medium | Low | 2nd |
| **Northern France** | High | High | 3rd |
| **Germany (border)** | Very High | Very High | Future |

### Netherlands Market Entry

#### Market Assessment

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Market size | €2B industrial supplies |
| Competition | Kramp dominant, fragmented local |
| Language | Dutch (same as Belgium) |
| Logistics | Easy from Belgian warehouse |
| Regulations | Similar to Belgium |

#### Entry Strategy

| Phase | Timeline | Actions |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Soft launch** | Month 22-24 | Enable NL shipping, Dutch SEO |
| **Active marketing** | Month 24-28 | Digital campaigns, trade shows |
| **Local presence** | Month 28-32 | Sales rep, local partnerships |
| **Full operations** | Month 32-36 | Consider local warehouse |

#### Netherlands Launch Checklist

- [ ] Website localization (NL-NL vs NL-BE)
- [ ] Shipping rates configured
- [ ] Payment methods (iDEAL)
- [ ] VAT registration
- [ ] Customer service hours
- [ ] Local phone number
- [ ] Dutch trade show presence (TechniShow, Agritechnica)

### France Pilot (Month 30-36)

#### Entry Considerations

| Factor | Challenge | Solution |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Language | French required | Already supported |
| Competition | Strong local players | Niche focus |
| Regulations | Different standards | Compliance review |
| Logistics | Longer distances | Partner or depot |

#### Pilot Approach

1. **Target:** Northern France (Lille, Dunkirk area)
2. **Focus:** Existing Belgian customer referrals
3. **Products:** High-margin, low-volume initially
4. **Investment:** Minimal until proven

## Workstream 3: Marketplace Features

### Marketplace Strategy (Month 20-24)

#### Business Model Options

| Model | Description | Pros | Cons |
|-------|-------------|------|------|
| **Curated marketplace** | Invite-only sellers | Quality control | Slower growth |
| **Open marketplace** | Any seller can join | Fast growth | Quality risk |
| **Hybrid** | Categories vary | Flexible | Complex |

**Recommendation:** Curated marketplace for complementary products

#### Marketplace Categories

| Category | Own Products | Marketplace |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Pumps | ✅ | ❌ |
| Valves | ✅ | ❌ |
| Safety equipment | ❌ | ✅ |
| Workwear | ❌ | ✅ |
| Specialty chemicals | ❌ | ✅ |
