## Part 11: Risk Mitigation

### Partner-Related Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Dealers don't sign up** | Medium | High | Strong value prop, pilot incentives |
| **Dealers don't reorder** | Medium | High | Onboarding support, check-ins |
| **Credit defaults** | Medium | Medium | Credit checks, limits, insurance |
| **Competitor response** | High | Medium | Differentiation, relationships |
| **Channel conflict** | High | High | Clear policies, separate products |

### Channel Conflict Management

**Rule 1:** DEMA B2B products are DIFFERENT from direct sale products
- B2B: Bulk, professional-grade, unbranded
- Direct: Retail packaging, DEMA branded

**Rule 2:** Geographic separation where possible
- Dealers get exclusive territories
- DEMA direct only in non-dealer areas

**Rule 3:** Price parity
- Dealer price + margin = DEMA direct price
- Dealers are never undercut
