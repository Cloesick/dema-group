## 1. Executive Summary

### Workstream Objective
Define DEMA Group's strategic direction, target operating model, and competitive positioning for the next 5 years, enabling transformation from fragmented family businesses to integrated B2B wholesale distribution platform.

### Key Questions Addressed

| Question | Our Recommendation |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Where to play?** | B2B wholesale distribution, Belgium/Benelux, pumps/irrigation/industrial niche |
| **How to win?** | Technical expertise + local service + digital platform |
| **What capabilities?** | Dealer network, inventory depth, e-commerce, technical support |
| **What management systems?** | Unified ERP, B2B platform, performance management |
