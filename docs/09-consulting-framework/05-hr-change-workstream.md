## 1. Executive Summary

### Workstream Objective
Design the future organization structure, build required capabilities, and manage the human side of DEMA Group's transformation to ensure successful adoption and sustainable change.

- Capability assessment & development plan
- Change management strategy
- Communication plan
- Training program
- Culture integration roadmap
