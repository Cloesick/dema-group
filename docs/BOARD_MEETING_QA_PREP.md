# DEMA Group Board Meeting - Q&A Preparation Document

## Digital Strategy & Platform Architecture

### Q: What is the overall digital transformation strategy for DEMA Group?
**A**: The strategy is multi-phased:
1. Phase 1 (Current): Unified B2B customer portal development
   - Single sign-on across all companies
   - Centralized product catalog
   - Integrated order management
2. Phase 2: Enhanced digital services
   - Predictive maintenance
   - IoT integration for industrial equipment
   - Real-time inventory management
3. Phase 3: AI and automation
   - Smart product recommendations
   - Automated order processing
   - Predictive analytics

### Q: Why choose a unified platform over individual company websites?
**A**: Key benefits include:
- Cost efficiency through shared infrastructure
- Consistent customer experience across brands
- Cross-selling opportunities between companies
- Simplified maintenance and updates
- Stronger market position as DEMA Group
- Better data analytics across all companies

## Technical Implementation

### Q: How will you handle data migration from existing systems?
**A**: The migration strategy involves:
1. Data Assessment
   - Audit of current databases
   - Data quality evaluation
   - Mapping of data structures
2. Migration Process
   - Phased migration by company
   - Automated data cleaning
   - Validation and testing
3. Timeline: 3-4 months per company
4. Zero downtime approach using parallel systems

### Q: What measures ensure platform security and reliability?
**A**:
- Azure Cloud infrastructure with geo-redundancy
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- GDPR compliance built into architecture
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures
- Multi-factor authentication for all users
